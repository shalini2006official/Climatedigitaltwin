# 🌍 HELIX – AI-Powered Climate Digital Twin

HELIX is an intelligent Climate Digital Twin platform that creates a virtual representation of real-world cities and regions to monitor environmental conditions, assess climate risks, simulate future scenarios, and provide early disaster warnings.

The system combines real-time weather data, geospatial visualization, predictive analytics, and AI-driven climate assessment to help governments, organizations, and communities make informed decisions before climate-related disasters occur.

## 🚀 Features

* 🌦 **Real-Time Climate Monitoring**

  * Temperature
  * Humidity
  * Wind Speed
  * Weather Conditions

* 🛰 **Digital Twin Visualization**

  * Interactive satellite map view
  * City-level monitoring
  * Risk zone highlighting

* 🤖 **AI Climate Assessment**

  * Automated climate risk analysis
  * Risk scoring and categorization
  * Environmental insights generation

* 📈 **Predictive Climate Simulation**

  * Future temperature forecasting
  * Humidity trend prediction
  * Climate scenario modeling

* 🚨 Early Warning System

  * Disaster risk detection
  * Climate alerts and notifications
  * Preventive recommendations

* 📊 Historical Analytics

  * Weather trend visualization
  * Environmental data history
  * Risk evolution tracking

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Climate disasters such as floods, heatwaves, droughts, and severe storms often cause significant damage due to delayed risk assessment and insufficient preparedness.

HELIX addresses this challenge by creating a digital twin of cities that continuously monitors climate conditions, predicts future impacts, identifies vulnerable areas, and recommends precautionary measures before disasters occur.
